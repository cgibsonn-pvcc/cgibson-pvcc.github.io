Considering both the benefits of the Internet to commerce and the financial vulnerabilities that Internet dependance has spawned, one question begs to be answered: “Has society become too dependent upon computer applications for commerce?” No inarguable stance exists regarding this inquiry. For my (the author of this website) part, I would answer this question with a resounding no, but you may beg to differ.
