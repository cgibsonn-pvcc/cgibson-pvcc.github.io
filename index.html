Commerce and the Internet


Every day, millions upon millions of financial transactions rebound across the globe via the Internet. From the real-time online trading of stocks, to the purchase of children's toys on Amazon, to certain types of subscriptions on 18+ websites, computer applications have revolutionized commerce. Yet despite these blessings to the global marketplace, the worldwide economy's dependence on computers represents a double-edged sword.
